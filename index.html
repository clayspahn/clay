Clay Spahn is an American graphic & type designer focusing on branding & editorial projects within cultural fields. He is currently the Creative Director at Bled (DSM) and a contract Designer with Now Now (DSM) and Emme (NYC).
  Clay’s self-initiated practice is devoted to typeface design and book publishing. In 2026, he will release his first retail typeface, CS Lucia.
